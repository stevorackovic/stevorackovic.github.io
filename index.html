I am a Ph.D. candidate at the Instituto Superior Técnico, enrolled in an industrial Ph.D. programme BIGMATH (This project has received funding from the European Union’s Horizon 2020 research and innovation programme under the Marie Skłodowska-Curie Grant Agreement No 812912). I work under the supervision of Cláudia Soares from NOVA Uni in Lisbon and Dušan Jakovetić from University of Novi Sad, and collaborate with 3Lateral Studio (Epic Games Company). The main focus of our research is applying machine learning and optimization techniques to improve the algorithms used in a 3D animation.

Below you can check some of the projects we've been working on.

Clustering of the Blendshape Facial Model

This is research on a facial segmentation for 3D animation that I did together with Cláudia Soares from IST (Lisbon), Dušan Jakovetić from PMF (Novi Sad), and Zoranka Desnica and Relja Ljubobratović from 3Laterl (Epic Games). I presented the work at EUSIPCO 2021 conference, and the paper is available in the conference proceedings.

Project 2

Accurate, Interpretable, and Fast Animation: An Iterative, Sparse, and Nonconvex Approach

I wrote this paper together with Cláudia Soares from IST (Lisbon), Dušan Jakovetić from PMF (Novi Sad), and Zoranka Desnica from 3Laterl (Epic Games). The main focus of the research is to develop a fast and accurate method for solving the inverse rig problem in facial animation. The paper is currently under submission for IEEE: Transactions on Signal Processing journal.
